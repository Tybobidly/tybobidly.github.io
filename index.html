About Us

Welcome to Whispering Roost Homestead—our family-run chicken farm in rural North Carolina. We raise heritage-breed hens on open pasture, hand-collect fresh eggs, and care deeply about sustainable farming. Every rooster, hen, and chick is part of the family.

From offering day-old chicks to sharing tips on coop design, our goal is to keep things honest, simple, and community-focused. Come see how we whisper to our roosters and watch our hens roam free.

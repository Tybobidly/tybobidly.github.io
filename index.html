Farm safety

Helpful guidance for safe, comfortable visits.

A few simple guidelines help protect the flock and make visits smoother for everyone.

Appointment-first visits

Visits should be scheduled ahead so the farm stays calm, organized, and easier to manage safely.

Biosecurity awareness

Guests should avoid recent outside poultry exposure before visiting to reduce health risks to the flock.

Children supervised

Young visitors should remain with an adult at all times around animals, equipment, and uneven ground.

Clear pickup instructions

Confirmed timing and directions help reduce confusion, traffic issues, and unnecessary disruption at the farm.

Suggested visit note

For the health and safety of our birds, visits are by appointment only. Please arrive in clean footwear, supervise children closely, and avoid visiting if you have had recent contact with outside poultry.
